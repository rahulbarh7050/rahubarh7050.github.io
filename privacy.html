Privacy Policy for Railway Quiz Pro

Effective Date: July 14, 2026

Developer: Rahul

This Privacy Policy explains how Railway Quiz Pro ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Railway Quiz Pro is designed to work primarily offline. The App itself does not require account creation, and does not collect or store personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number on our servers.

The App stores certain data locally on your device only, including:

  • Your quiz progress, scores, and answer history
  • App preferences and settings

This local data is never transmitted to us and remains on your device unless you uninstall the App, which will erase it.

2. Advertising

Railway Quiz Pro displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a service provided by Google LLC. To deliver ads, including personalized ads, AdMob and its partners may collect and use certain information such as:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address and general location (country/region level)
  • Device type, operating system, and app usage data related to ad interactions

This data collection and processing is governed by Google's own privacy policies. You can learn more about how Google uses this data here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from AdMob

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

3. Internet Connectivity

The App checks your device's network connectivity status (via the connectivity_plus package) solely to determine whether ads or online features can be loaded. This does not involve collecting or transmitting personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

Aside from Google AdMob as described above, Railway Quiz Pro does not integrate any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or account/authentication services at this time.

5. Children's Privacy

Railway Quiz Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

6. Data Security

Since the App does not transmit personal data to our own servers, most information relevant to your usage remains stored locally and securely on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
